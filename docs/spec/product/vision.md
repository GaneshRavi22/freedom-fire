# Product Vision

## Product Name
**FreedomFire** (package: `com.freedomfire.app`)
Tagline: *"Retire Early. Live Free."*

## Mission
Make FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) planning feel like a game, not a spreadsheet.
Transform the abstract goal of "build ₹X crore corpus" into a daily habit of small, rewarding
actions — through gamification, personalised insights, and an AI advisor that knows your numbers.

## The Core Insight
The biggest reason people don't plan for FIRE is emotional distance: "₹4.5 crore" means nothing.
"You've earned 47 Freedom Days" means something. FreedomFire reframes every rupee saved as a
concrete unit of time — days you will never have to work again.

## Target Users
**Primary:** Indian salaried professionals, 25–40 years old, with:
- A stable monthly income (₹50k–₹3L/month range)
- At least one EMI (home/car/education loan) — very common in India
- Awareness of FIRE but overwhelmed by the complexity
- A smartphone (Android primary; iOS secondary)

**Not targeting:**
- Day traders or active investors
- People below ₹30k/month (FIRE math doesn't work)
- Retirees (no accumulation phase)

## Problems Solved

| Problem | How FreedomFire Solves It |
|---------|--------------------------|
| "My FIRE number feels impossible" | Shows exact retire-at age and years away, updated live |
| "I don't know where my money goes" | PDF statement upload → automatic categorization in seconds |
| "EMI is ruining my future" | Dedicated EMI impact tab shows exactly how many years loan costs |
| "I know I should save more but I don't" | Gamification: XP, streaks, badges, quests drive daily habits |
| "I don't know what to actually *do*" | AI-generated personalized tasks with FIRE impact shown |
| "I can't afford a financial advisor" | AI advisor tab — ask anything, get answers from your real data |

## Non-Goals
- **Not a budgeting app** — no manual transaction entry, no budget envelopes
- **Not a brokerage** — no stock/mutual fund purchase flows
- **Not a robo-advisor** — no automated investing or portfolio rebalancing
- **Not a bank aggregator** — no live bank/UPI feed integration (PDF upload only, v1)
- **Not multi-currency** — INR only

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| D7 retention | > 40% |
| Statements uploaded per active user | > 1 in first week |
| Task acceptance rate | > 50% of recommended tasks |
| FIRE calc update frequency | > 1× per week per active user |
| Streak retention (7-day) | > 30% of users hit 7-day streak |

## Brand Voice
- Warm, direct, slightly playful — not corporate
- Uses Indian financial context naturally (EMI, SIP, crore/lakh)
- Celebrates small wins loudly (confetti, XP toasts)
- Never shames spending patterns — frames cuts as "unlocking freedom"

## Platform Priority
1. Android (primary — India market share)
2. iOS (secondary)
3. Web — not planned for v1
