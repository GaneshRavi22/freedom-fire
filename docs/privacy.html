Privacy Policy

Effective date: 28 May 2026  |  Last updated: 28 May 2026

FreedomFire ("we", "our", "us") is a personal finance app that helps you plan for financial independence. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, age (provided during sign-up).
  • Financial planning data: monthly income, monthly expenses, savings rate, loan EMI details, target retirement age, and FIRE corpus calculations you save in the app.
  • Spending data: transaction categories derived from credit card PDF statements you optionally upload. We extract category totals; we do not store the raw PDF after processing.
  • Usage data: in-app events (screens viewed, features used, badges unlocked) collected for product analytics. These events are stored in our database and visualised in an internal Grafana dashboard.
  • Device data: basic device and OS information collected by Sentry for crash reporting.

2. How we use your data

  • To compute and display your FIRE number, wealth timeline, and personalised tasks.
  • To power the gamification system (XP, levels, badges, streaks, quests).
  • To generate spending insights from uploaded credit card statements.
  • To understand how the app is used so we can improve it.
  • To diagnose crashes and performance issues via Sentry.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage. Data is stored on servers in the EU (AWS eu-west-1). Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in method. If you sign in with Google, Google processes your Google account data under Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry — crash reporting. Sentry receives device metadata and anonymised stack traces when the app crashes. Sentry Privacy Policy.

4. Data retention

Your account and financial data are retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 6). Anonymised analytics events may be retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Financial planning data is stored in a Postgres database with row-level security — each user can only access their own rows. Uploaded credit card PDFs are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket and deleted after processing.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or export your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ganesh.reddy.22@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children

FreedomFire is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
